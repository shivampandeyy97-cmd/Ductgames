About Ductgames

On a mission to bring free gaming joy to everyone, everywhere

Ductgames was founded in 2025 by Alex Carter, a London-based indie developer who was frustrated by the number of free game sites that were either riddled with intrusive ads, required sign-ups, or pushed low-quality games. Alex built the first four games himself over a weekend and launched quietly. The site grew organically from players who shared it with friends. Our mission has always been simple: build beautiful, fast, genuinely free games that respect the player.

Who We Are

Hello! I'm Alex Carter, the founder of Ductgames, a web development studio and instant gaming platform. Based in London, UK, Ductgames was established in 2025 because I wanted to create a clean, friendly corner of the internet where people could play high-quality games without any barriers. As a game developer, I grew frustrated watching modern casual gaming get weighed down by massive app store downloads, intrusive account sign-ups, and aggressive paywalls that ruin the fun. I believed we could do better by going back to the golden era of browser gaming—where you simply click a link and start playing immediately.

Today, Ductgames is a dedicated passion project focused on building and hosting responsive, premium HTML5 games like Tic Tac Toe, Snake, 2048, and Memory Match. We code these experiences with speed and clean gameplay in mind, ensuring they run flawlessly on everything from an old mobile phone to a modern high-resolution desktop screen. We also work hard behind the scenes to regularly update our library with new features, clean designs, and smart AI opponents. We're a small, independent team committed to keeping gaming pure, accessible, and 100% free for everyone, everywhere. This is gaming the way it was meant to be: instant, lightweight, and entirely focused on having fun. Thank you for visiting, and I hope our games bring a little bit of quick joy to your day!

Our Mission

Our mission is straightforward: to make free, fun, and fair gaming accessible to everyone on the internet. We are committed to:

  • Zero Barriers: No downloads, no accounts, no paywalls. Just click and play.
  • Quality Over Quantity: Every game is carefully selected and tested to ensure a great experience.
  • Cross-Platform Play: All our games are built with HTML5 and work on any modern browser or device.
  • Safe Gaming Environment: We maintain a safe, family-friendly platform with no inappropriate content.
  • Privacy Respect: We handle your data responsibly and never sell your personal information.

What We Offer

Ductgames hosts a growing library of free HTML5 games across multiple genres:

  • Puzzle Games: Brain-teasing logic games, including Tic Tac Toe, 2048, Memory Match, and Sudoku.
  • Arcade Games: Fast-paced classic action featuring Snake.

New games are added regularly, so there's always something fresh to explore on Ductgames.

How We're Free

Ductgames is 100% free because we are supported by advertising revenue. We display non-intrusive advertisements through trusted networks including Google AdSense. This allows us to keep all games completely free for our users while continuing to invest in new game additions and platform improvements. We are committed to maintaining a clean, respectful ad experience and will never show ads that mislead, deceive, or disrespect our users.

Our Values

  • Transparency: We are open about how our platform works, how we make money, and how we use your data.
  • Inclusivity: Gaming is for everyone. Our platform is designed to be welcoming to all ages and backgrounds.
  • Integrity: We do not engage in click-bait, deceptive advertising, or unfair practices.
  • Continuous Improvement: We listen to our users and continuously work to improve the platform.

Our Team

Our Team is led by Alex Carter, the founder and lead developer of Ductgames. With over a decade of experience in software engineering and web development, Alex is passionate about keeping the web open, fast, and fun. He designs, codes, and maintains the platform from his home office in London, UK. Alex's goal is to expand the Ductgames library to 50 high-quality, free browser games by the end of 2026, continuing to build experiences that respect player privacy.

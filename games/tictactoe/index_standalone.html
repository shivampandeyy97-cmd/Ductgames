About Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe (also known as Noughts and Crosses) is one of the world's most beloved classic strategy games. Play as X against our AI computer opponent that uses a minimax algorithm to challenge you on both Easy and Hard difficulty settings. Get three of your marks in a row — horizontally, vertically, or diagonally — to win. All you need is your browser, no download or registration required!

How to Play

1
The board is a 3×3 grid. You are X, the computer is O.
2
Click any empty cell on your turn to place your mark.
3
Get 3 of your marks in a row — across, down, or diagonally — to win!
4
If all 9 cells are filled with no winner, it's a draw.

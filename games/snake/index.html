About Snake

Snake is a timeless arcade game that has been entertaining players since the 1970s. Control the snake using your arrow keys or WASD controls, eat food to grow longer, and avoid crashing into the walls or yourself. With multiple speed settings from Slow to Fast, this free browser version of Snake offers endless replayability. No download or registration needed — just pure retro gaming fun!

How to Play

1
Use Arrow Keys or WASD to control the snake's direction.
2
Eat the red food to grow longer and score points.
3
Avoid hitting the walls or your own tail.
4
The longer you survive, the higher your score!

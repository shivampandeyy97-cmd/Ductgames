About 2048

2048 is an addictive sliding tile puzzle game. Use your arrow keys (or WASD) to slide numbered tiles on a 4×4 grid. When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one! Your goal is to reach the 2048 tile. Features undo, high-score tracking, and mobile swipe support. Free to play — no download, no registration needed.

How to Play

1
Use Arrow Keys or WASD to slide all tiles in that direction.
2
When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one.
3
A new tile (2 or 4) appears after every move.
4
Reach the 2048 tile to win — or keep going for a high score!

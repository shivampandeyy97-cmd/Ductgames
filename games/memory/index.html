About Memory Match

Memory Match is a classic card-flipping concentration game that challenges your visual memory and recall. Flip cards two at a time to find matching emoji pairs. The game features three difficulty levels — 4×4 (16 cards), 4×5 (20 cards), and 5×6 (30 cards) — with a built-in timer and move counter to track your performance. Challenge yourself to beat your best score! Free to play, no download or registration needed.

How to Play

1
Click any card to flip it over and reveal the emoji.
2
Click a second card to see if it matches the first.
3
If they match, they stay face-up. If not, both flip back!
4
Find all pairs with the fewest moves to win!
